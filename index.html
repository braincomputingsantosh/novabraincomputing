Neuroscience Talk Sessions

Welcome to NOVA Brain Computing's Neuroscience Talk Sessions – a premier forum where leading researchers, scientists, and enthusiasts gather to explore the latest breakthroughs in neuroscience and brain-inspired computing.


Our monthly talk sessions bring together interdisciplinary experts to share insights on neural networks, computational neuroscience, brain-computer interfaces, and the future of intelligent systems. Join us as we decode the mysteries of the brain and translate them into revolutionary computing paradigms.
